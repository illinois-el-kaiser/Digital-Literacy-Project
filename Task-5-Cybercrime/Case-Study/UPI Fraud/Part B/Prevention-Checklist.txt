Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Tips:

- Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or banking details with anyone
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links received via SMS, email, or WhatsApp
- Always verify the identity of the sender before accepting any payment request
- Remember that receiving money does not require entering a PIN or OTP
- Use strong, unique passwords for all your accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Check website URLs carefully before entering sensitive information
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Keep your mobile apps and operating system updated
- Install apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or App Store
- Immediately report any suspicious activity on https://cybercrime.gov.in
- In case of fraud, call the national cybercrime helpline: 1930
